About IDR

The Institute of Digital Risk (IDR) is an industry-led training and deployment institute focused on digital, cyber and technology risk roles in regulated and high-consequence environments.

Through collaboration with academic partners and industry leaders, IDR bridges the gap between academic research and real-world implementation, enabling professionals to gain practical experience while organizations gain access to skilled digital risk talent.

Our Model

Academy

Training programs and bootcamps for students and professionals in digital risk, cybersecurity, and technology governance.

Innovation & Incubation

Developing new risk models, governance frameworks, and AI risk management solutions through research and collaboration.

Advisory Services

Supporting organizations in implementing frameworks such as NIST, ISO 27001, and NIS2 compliance standards.

Community

IDR serves students, early-career professionals, and experienced practitioners seeking to build careers in digital and cyber risk.

Our programs are designed for sectors including financial services, government, and critical infrastructure organizations that require strong risk management and security expertise.
